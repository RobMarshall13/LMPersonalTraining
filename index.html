Buddy Training

Find the gym a little intimidating? do you feel anxious training on your own? why not buddy train? Spread the cost and double the fun! Help motivate each other through your fitness journey, giving you that extra support to train together between sessions, in turn increasing your commitment towards your goal.

Small Group Training (Maximum of 6)

When you join a group of people with similar goals and aspirations you will benefit from the energy and camaraderie small group training can offer. Although these sessions are group orientated they will still be tailored towards your individual needs and abilities.
